Questions

Do students need accounts?

No. Figura is a tool for teachers to make diagrams. Students never log in and no pupil data is collected.

Can I use the diagrams in my own exams and sell resources?

Yes — you own the diagrams you create and can use them freely in your teaching and resources.

How do school licences work?

A single licence covers your whole maths department, billed annually by invoice or purchase order. Get in touch for a quote.

Is my work saved?

Today, work is saved in your browser. Cloud saving that syncs across devices is coming with Pro accounts.

Give teachers their evenings back

Figura exists for one reason: making a maths diagram should take seconds, not half an hour.

Every maths teacher knows the feeling. It's Sunday evening, the assessment is due tomorrow, and you just need one clean right-angled triangle with the sides labelled. Twenty minutes later you're still wrestling with Word's shape tools, screenshotting from other software, or drawing it by hand and hoping it photocopies.

The tools that exist are either too general — they don't understand maths — or too powerful, built for interactive exploration rather than a quick, static, print-ready figure. Neither fits the actual job.

A tool that speaks maths

Figura is built specifically for the diagrams maths teachers make every week: labelled shapes and angles, graphs, statistics charts, bearings, circle theorems, probability trees, Venn diagrams, number lines, 3D solids and more. Pick a tool, click, label with a built-in maths keyboard, and export a crisp, transparent image straight into your exam or worksheet.

No accounts to set up, no learning curve, no clutter. Just the diagram you need, ready in under a minute.
